Winners of the Knight News Challenge on Data

January 26, 2015

Our project (full proposal) was one of winners of John S. and James L. Knight Foundation's Knight News Challenge. Our project's goal is to give people a chance to examine the possibilities and ethics of our society's future with technology. We use theatre and interactive art to bring people into a world where their data has been integrated into the creation to shed light on how our own data can be used for research and commercial products. Through this engaging and personal approach, we want to start and continue conversations about data ethics and social issues.
