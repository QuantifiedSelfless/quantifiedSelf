Overview

We are an immersive theater and interactive art project currently housed at University of Colorado in the Department of Computer Science. Our project received support from the John S. and James L. Knight Foundationâ€™s Knight News Challenge and CU-Boulder's Enginnering Excellence Fund.

Our project's goal is to engage a broad audience of people who are interested in the future and ethics of data sharing online. More than simply privacy, we aim to show people the possibilities around product development, research, artificial intelligence, and human inference given the amounts of data owned by third-parties. To achieve this, we ask people to share their data with us for the duration of the show so that we can meet guests at the level of their actual lives, only to then take them somewhere else to expand their imagination.

Our first show will happen April 28 - May 1, 2016 in Boulder, CO at ATLAS's Blackbox Theater. We intend to move the show into other locations and run public events that open public dialogue around the ethical considerations we have placed in the performance. If you are interested in seeing this production come to your location or to collaborate on a run of it, please contact our project lead at: michael[dot]skirpan[at]colorado[dot]edu
